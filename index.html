GTA 6 Release Date Scheduled for November 19th 2026

Rockstar Games have announced that the highly anticipated sixth edition of Grand Theft Auto will be released later this year on the 19th of November 2026. The game was initially meant to be released in the Summer of 2026, however like several times before the release date was pushed back to ensure readjustments could be made on time. This had left a lot of fans disappointed as the release of GTA 6 in the summer would’ve been great for during summer holidays and the 2026 World Cup.

GTA 6 will mainly be set in the fictional state Leonida which is based on the state of Florida and the main city will be Vice City which is meant to imitate Miami.
